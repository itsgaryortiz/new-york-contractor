What to Look for in a New York Contractor

  • Experience: A reputable contractor should have extensive experience in home renovations and remodeling.
  • Licensing and Insurance: Always check that your contractor is licensed and carries insurance to protect you and your property.
  • References: Ask for references and review past projects to ensure their work meets your expectations.
